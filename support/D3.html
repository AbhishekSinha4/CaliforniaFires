View a County or Compare 2 Counties

Interactive Plot using D3

Due to the high number of counties present in the California data, we are not able to show the damages caused by the fires in all counties nor compare all of them easily through static graphs. Thus we create an interactive plot that allows a user to select 1 or 2 county(s) and look at them individually or compare the damage each of them has suffered (in area burned) over time due to various fire causes.
  1. Choose a county from the dropdown (by default, all causes are selected). This will render a graph for the county.
  2. Choose a second county (if desired) from the County 2 dropdown. This will render a graph for the 2nd county next to the first one.
  3. Deselect/select any of the fire causes listed to filter the causes being compared between the two counties in the graph below.
  4. The graph dynamically updates itself as we change counties or change the fire causes being compared.
